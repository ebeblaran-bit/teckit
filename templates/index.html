About Us

TICK.IT is a Cinema ticketing system that allows you to book a ticket to watch movies in cinema. The Admin controls updating what movies are available this week and others. Venues include SM Seaside Cebu Cinema, Gaisano Grand Minglanilla Cinema, Nustar Cebu Cinema, Cebu IL CORSO Cinema, UC Cantao-an, and TOPS Cebu Skydom.
